Mobile Legend

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena game developed and
published by Moonton, a subsidiary of ByteDance.
Released in 2016, the game grew in popularity worldwide, most prominently in Southeast Asia,
and has since crossed 1 billion downloads, with peak monthly players of 100 million.
